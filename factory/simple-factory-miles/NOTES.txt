Title: The Factory Pattern In Go
URL: https://levelup.gitconnected.com/the-factory-pattern-in-go-a584a9467ffd


In general, you should think of using the Factory pattern when you 
need some object that could be dependent on other objects. 

Localizing the logic of a complex object is another ideal situation 
for Factory pattern, but it’s important to make sure you don’t 
over-abstract your program
